Our Story

Built on resilience and courage

RIZOK is two words made one — Resilience and Isog, the Bisaya word for courage. It's the spirit we were raised on, stitched into everything we make.

ResilienceStrength that endures
IsogBisaya for courage

The brand was born from a simple frustration: quality activewear shouldn't cost a fortune. Every time we wanted leggings that actually performed — squat-proof, comfortable all day, with a pocket that holds your phone — we ended up paying for the label, not the leggings.

Rizok is different. Premium fabric, a high-waist compressive fit, and a price that respects you.

You're not here to blend in. You're here to become everything you're capable of.

How to measure

Waist — measure around the narrowest part of your natural waist, just above the belly button. Keep the tape snug but not tight.

Hips — measure around the fullest part of your hips and glutes. Stand with feet together for accuracy.

Sizing tips

  • If you're between sizes or prefer a more relaxed fit, size up.
